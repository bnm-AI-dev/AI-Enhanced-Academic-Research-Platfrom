How to Write a Research Paper

A step-by-step guide to writing a research paper

Step 1: Understand the Assignment

Read and Understand the Assignment

Before you begin your research, it is important to understand the assignment. Read the assignment prompt carefully and make sure you know exactly what is expected of you. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to ask your instructor for clarification.

Read the assignment prompt

2 hours

Ask your instructor for clarification

1 hour

Step 2: Choose a Topic

Choose a Topic

Choosing a topic is an important part of the research process. You should choose a topic that interests you and that you can research effectively. Consider the length of the paper, the requirements of the assignment, and the availability of sources when choosing a topic.

Brainstorm potential topics

2 hours

Research each topic to determine availability of sources

1 hour

Step 3: Research and Develop a Thesis

Research and Develop a Thesis

Once you have chosen a topic, you will need to conduct research to gather information and evidence. As you conduct your research, you should develop a thesis statement that clearly and concisely summarizes the main point or argument of your paper.

Conduct research and gather evidence

4 hours

Develop a thesis statement

2 hours

Step 4: Create an Outline and Draft

Create an Outline and Draft

With your thesis statement in mind, you can create an outline to organize your ideas and ensure that your paper flows logically. Once you have an outline, you can start drafting your paper. Remember to use your research to support your thesis and include citations for any sources you use.

Create an outline

3 hours

Draft your paper

6 hours
